Hauling Services

Day Trips

Hauling can be stressful for horses and owners alike, even when it's just a short trip to a show or moving to a new barn. As horse owners ourselves, we understand how stressful it can be. We want you to know that even on a brief trip, your horse will always receive the absolute best care and support when traveling with us.

Overnight Trips

After a day of travel you can rest assured that your horse will be settled into a comfortable stall at one of our layover barns. Once there they can stretch, get something to eat, rest, and be ready to go first thing in the morning. And because your peace of mind is just as important, we will keep you informed every step of the way!

Long Distance Trips

For long-distance trips, your horse will receive regular feeding and watering and opportunities to stretch during the trip. At the end of each night, they will have a comfortable stall at one of our layover barns. As always, we will keep you informed along the way, ensuring peace of mind for both of you throughout the trip.

Our Equipment

We use 2024 model 4-star trailers, equipped with air ride suspension, insulated roofs, three electric exhaust fans and five cooling fans to maintain a cooler, more comfortable, and temperature-controlled environment for your horse.
Our trailers are video monitored and routinely maintained to ensure maximum safety, reliability and performance. We reinvest in our equipment and operations to stay ahead of your evolving needs - because your horse deserves the best.
